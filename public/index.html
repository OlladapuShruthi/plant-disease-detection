About PlantPulse

At PlantPulse, we aim to revolutionize plant healthcare through AI-powered diagnostics. Our mission is to help farmers, gardeners, and plant enthusiasts easily detect plant diseases and receive treatment recommendations.

Plant diseases can significantly affect crop yield and plant health, but early detection and accurate identification can help prevent major losses. By utilizing our AI technology, users can diagnose diseases in plants quickly and accurately, reducing the time and effort required to manually inspect each plant.
